How this works

It already runs on best-guess numbers. Open Setup once when you have your statements, correct anything, and it gets truer. Each field is an estimate until you confirm it.

Tune any month's spending with its sliders — the finish date up top moves as you drag. Check off each payment as you make it; the ledger shows you're never short.

Back it up anytime — balances, budgets, entries, and check-marks all save and reload.
